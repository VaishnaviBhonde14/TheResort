About The Location

The Resort Mumbai is strategically located along a serene beach, offering a peaceful retreat within easy reach of the city's main points of interest. Whether you're visiting for business or leisure, travelling here and to nearby attractions is simple and convenient.

We are just 400 meters from Aksa Beach - perfect for a morning stroll or evening relaxation. The iconic Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj International Airport is only 21.1 km away, making both arrival and departure effortless. Borivali, Railway Station, 11 km away, provides excellent local train connectivity. Guests also have the chance to visit local attractions such as Madh Fort, which is just 4.7 km away, and Marve Beach, a mere 3.4 km from the resort. The Resort Mumbai keeps you close to it all, while offering the peace and seclusion of a beachside getaway.
